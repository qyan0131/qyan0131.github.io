About

I am a postdoctoral fellow at Tongji University, working with Prof. Qijun Chen in the Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Lab. My research focuses on robot perception, real-time semantic segmentation, vision-language navigation, and trustworthy autonomous systems. I received my Ph.D. from Tongji University under the supervision of Prof. Chengju Liu.

Recent highlights: Youth Talent Support Program for PhD Students by CAST (2024), Shanghai Super Postdoctoral Program (2025), Shanghai Outstanding Graduate (2024), and Best Cognitive Paper Finalist at IROS 2024.
